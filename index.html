About Us

Dr V's Swing Thing is a premier Big Band based in Sydney, Australia. We play an ecclectic mix of genres, ranging from Count Basie swing to latin and world music. We perform regularly in Lazybones Lounge in Marrickville, Jazz Festivals in Wangaratta, Darwin, Hobart and Manly, and the Angel Place City Recital Hall in Sydney. We are available for bookings at weddings and other private functions.
